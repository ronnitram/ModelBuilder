Description

TextEngine can decompose richtexts to parts that that have the same style and surely fit the same line previously telling their sytles and position for writing so it is easy to just put them down on a screen or any other device. IPlainTextBuilder is an iterface for a class hangling the calls from the textengine with the sytle, position and text of each such piece of thext mentioned above. Calling any methods for decomposing texts (e.g. DecomposeRichText) requires the implementation of a class that evaluates this IPlainTextBuilder interface. During a whole text decomposing process one after the other all the following methods will be called by textengine in this given order: BeginText with the information of the angle of the output, then for every change in the style BeginRun with the style, and for each piece of text with the same style OneRun is called having the text, the baseline position and the measured width of this piece of text in its argument. Note that for the same style calls for many text runs may come as texts broke at the end of the lines are given separately and even in the same line may be numerous runs of texts split apart due to the attributes of their characters. At the end of the whole text the EndText is called.
