PlaneEq

Plane defined with the following equation: pa*x + pb*y + pc*z + pd = 0.

    typedef struct struct {
      double  pa;
      double  pb;
      double  pc;
      double  pd;
    } PlaneEq;

Members

pa
Coefficient of x in the defining equation of the plane.
 
pb
Coefficient of y in the defining equation of the plane.
 
pc
Coefficient of z in the defining equation of the plane.
 
pd
Coefficient of the constant term in the defining equation of the plane.
 

Requirements

Version: 1.0 or later
Header: Plane3DData.h
