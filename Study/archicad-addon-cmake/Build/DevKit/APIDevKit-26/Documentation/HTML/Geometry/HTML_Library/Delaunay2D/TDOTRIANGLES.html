Geometry::TDOTRIANGLES

This structure is to hold a triangle in the triangulation.

    struct TDOTRIANGLES {
      short         filler1;
      short         status;
      long          edges[3];
    };

Members

filler1
Reserved.
 
status
Status of the triangle.
 
edges[3]
Indices of the three bounding edges with positive orientation.
 

Requirements

Version: 1.0 or later
