GenArc

Describes a general arc. It can be a full circle, an arc of a circle, a full ellipse or an arc of an ellipse.

    struct GenArc {
      Coord   origC;
      double  r;
      double  angle;
      double  ratio;
      double  begAng;
      double  endAng;
      Coord   begC;
      Coord   endC;
      bool    elliptic;
      bool    reflected;
      bool    whole;
      bool    filler_1;
      long    filler_2;
    };

Members

origC
The center of the arc.
 
r
The radius of the arc in case of a circle or circle arc. One of the axes in case of an ellipse or ellipse arc.
 
angle
Unused in case of circle or circle arc. The direction angle of the above axis in case of an ellipse or ellipse arc.
 
ratio
Unused in case of circle or circle arc. The ratio of the above and the secondary axis in case of an ellipse or ellipse arc.
 
begAng
Unused in case of circle or ellipse. The beginning direction in case of circle arc or elliptic arc.
 
endAng
Unused in case of circle or ellipse. The ending direction in case of circle arc or elliptic arc.
 
begC
Unused in case of circle or ellipse. The point on the curve in the beginning direction in case of circle arc or elliptic arc.
 
endC
Unused in case of circle or ellipse. The point on the curve in the ending direction in case of circle arc or elliptic arc.
 
elliptic
True if it is an ellipse or ellipse arc.
 
reflected
True if its orientation is negative.
 
whole
True in case of circle or ellipse.
 
filler_1
Reserved.
 
filler_2
Reserved.
