Geometry::TMCalcArc

Calculates the points of a transformed arc.

     void TMCalcArc (
      double    x0,
      double    y0,
      double    r1,
      double    r2,
      double    alpha,
      double    beta,
      long*     ncs,
      TRANMAT*  tran,
      double*   coords
    );

Parameters

x0
x coordinate of the center of the arc.
y0
y coordinate of the center of the arc.
r1
Radius at the beginning of the arc.
r2
Radius at the end of the arc.
alpha
Angle of the beginning of the arc.
beta
Angle of the end of the arc.
ncs
Number of the points of the transformed arc to calculate.
tran
Transformation matrix.
coords
Coordinates of the points of the transformed arc.

Requirements

Version: 1.0 or later
Header: TM.h
Import Library: GeometryImp.lib [Win]
Module: Geometry.dll [WIN] GeometryLib [Mac]
