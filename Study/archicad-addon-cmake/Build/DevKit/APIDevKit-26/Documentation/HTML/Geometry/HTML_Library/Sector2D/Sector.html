Sector

Represents a sector a ray or a line by two points on them. The represented items are always directed, so the two endpoints are not interchangeable.

    struct Sector {
      Coord  c1;
      Coord  c2;
    };

Members

c1
Coordinates of the first point of the represented item..
 
c2
Coordinates of the second point of the represented item.
 

Remarks

  • In case of a sector, c1 is the beginning point, c2 is the end point.
  • In case of a ray, c1 is the strating point, c2 is an arbitrary point of the ray.
  • In case of a line, both c1 and c2 are arbitrary points of the line.

Requirements

Version: 1.0 or later
Header: Sector2DData.h
