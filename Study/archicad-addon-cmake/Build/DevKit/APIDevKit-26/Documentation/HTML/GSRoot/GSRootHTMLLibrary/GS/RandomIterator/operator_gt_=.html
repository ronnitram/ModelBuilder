RandomIterator<Type>::operator>=

Iterator comparision (greater than or equal). 

bool operator>= (const RandomIterator & rightOp) const;

Parameters

rightOp
Right operand of the comparison operation.

Return Value

Value Meaning
true this iterator refers to an item that is after or equal of the item referred to by the other iterator
false this iterator refers to an item that is not after or equal of the item referred to by the other iterator

Description

This operator can compare two iterators to determine whether this iterator refers to an item that is after or equal of the item referred to by the other iterator.
